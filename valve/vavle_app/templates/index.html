About Us

M/S ARVHI METAL ARTS, established in the year 1998, is a fast growing Valve Industry located in Hubli (Karnataka) & growing across INDIA.

M/S ARVHI METAL ARTS is well established & is a professionally expert valve manufacturer. We are manufacturing & marketing products with high level of quality, reliability, precision valued products & services. We offer a wide range of NEEDLE, BALL, SEALANT VALVES, PIPE CONNECTORS, DRAIN PLUGS, valve components & precision automobile machined components which are mainly applied in the field of petroleum, gas, chemical industries, metallurgy, offshore, earth moving equipments etc.
